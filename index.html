Welcome to PoliPick 2024! This website is designed to help you pick your candidate for the upcoming election. There are topics and two options. Choose the option that resonates most with your morals. Each question has an input of weight so you can put importance on the topics you care about the most. After choosing the importance and each option for each topic, press calculate to see which candidate is the best fit for you.
